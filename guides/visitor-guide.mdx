## Submitting Testimonials

Share your Zoo Arcadia experience with other visitors!

### How to Submit a Testimonial

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit After Your Experience">
    Navigate to the testimonials section (usually found in the footer or contact page).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Out the Form">
    Provide:
    - **Pseudonym**: Your name or nickname (how you want to be identified)
    - **Rating**: 1-5 stars (5 being excellent)
    - **Message**: Your experience in your own words
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Your Review">
    Share details about:
    - What you enjoyed most
    - Favorite animals or exhibits
    - Staff interactions
    - Suggestions for improvement (constructive feedback welcome)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit for Review">
    Click Submit. Your testimonial will be reviewed by our team before appearing publicly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All testimonials are moderated to ensure appropriate content. This process usually takes less than 24 hours.
</Note>

### Testimonial Guidelines

To ensure your testimonial is approved:

**Do:**
- Be honest and constructive
- Share specific experiences
- Mention staff by name (if positive)
- Include details that help other visitors
- Rate fairly based on your experience

**Don't:**
- Use inappropriate language
- Include spam or advertising
- Post off-topic content
- Submit fake reviews
- Include personal contact information (for your privacy)

### Rating Guide

- **5 Stars**: Excellent - Exceeded expectations
- **4 Stars**: Very Good - Highly enjoyable experience
- **3 Stars**: Good - Met expectations
- **2 Stars**: Fair - Some disappointments
- **1 Star**: Poor - Significant issues

<Tip>
  The most helpful testimonials are specific! Instead of "Great zoo," try "The new penguin exhibit was amazing - my kids loved the underwater viewing tunnel!"
</Tip>
