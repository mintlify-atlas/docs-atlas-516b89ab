## Overview

As a Zoo Arcadia employee, you play a crucial role in daily animal care and visitor experience management. This guide covers your primary responsibilities: recording animal feedings, moderating visitor testimonials, and updating service information.

## Employee Dashboard

Your dashboard at `/home/pages/start` provides quick access to:

- Recent feeding logs you've created
- Pending testimonials requiring moderation
- Service update notifications
- Contact form submissions from visitors
- Your recent activity summary

<Tip>
  Start each shift by checking your dashboard to see pending tasks and recent updates.
</Tip>

## Managing Feeding Logs

Accurate feeding records are essential for animal health monitoring and veterinary care.

### Viewing All Feeding Logs

Navigate to `/animals/feeding/start` to see all feeding records:

- Animal name and species
- Food type provided
- Quantity (in grams)
- Feeding date and time
- Employee who performed the feeding

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Filter by Animal" icon="filter">
    Click on an animal to view its complete feeding history.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Recent Feedings" icon="clock">
    Sort by date to see the most recent feedings first.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Recording Animal Feedings

Every time you feed an animal, create a feeding log entry.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Feeding Log Creation">
    Go to `/animals/feeding/create` or click "Record Feeding" from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select the Animal">
    Choose which animal you fed from the dropdown list. Animals are organized by name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Food Type">
    Select the type of food provided:
    - **Meat**: For carnivores
    - **Fruit**: For frugivores and some omnivores
    - **Legumes**: For herbivores
    - **Insect**: For insectivores and some omnivores
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Quantity">
    Record the amount of food in grams. Be accurate - this data is used by veterinarians to monitor animal health.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Feeding Date">
    Enter the date of feeding. This defaults to today but can be changed if you're recording a past feeding.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click Save. The log is automatically attributed to your user account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The quantity must be a positive number greater than 0. The system validates this to ensure data accuracy.
</Note>

### Viewing Feeding History by Animal

To see all feedings for a specific animal:

1. Go to `/animals/feeding/start`
2. Click on the animal's name
3. View complete feeding history at `/animals/feeding/view?animal_id={id}`

This view shows:

- All feeding records chronologically
- Total food provided over time
- Last feeding date and details
- Feeding pattern analysis

### Deleting Feeding Logs

If you recorded a feeding in error:

1. Navigate to the feeding log list
2. Click "Delete" next to the incorrect entry
3. Confirm deletion

<Warning>
  Only delete feeding logs that were recorded by mistake. Accurate records are essential for veterinary care.
</Warning>

## Moderating Testimonials

Visitors can submit testimonials through the public website. As an employee, you review and moderate these submissions before they appear publicly.

### Accessing Testimonial Management

Navigate to `/testimonials/gest/start` to see all testimonials:

- Pending (awaiting moderation)
- Validated (approved and visible to public)
- Rejected (not approved for publication)

<Tip>
  Use the status filter at the top to view only pending testimonials that need your attention.
</Tip>

### Validating Testimonials

To approve a testimonial for public display:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Content">
    Read the testimonial carefully:
    - Check for appropriate language
    - Verify the content is relevant to the zoo
    - Ensure there's no spam or promotional content
    - Confirm the rating (1-5 stars) matches the message tone
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Validate">
    If the testimonial is appropriate, click the "Validate" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    The testimonial status changes to "Validated" and it becomes visible on the public website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Rejecting Testimonials

Reject testimonials that contain:

- Inappropriate language or content
- Spam or advertising
- Off-topic comments
- Fake or obviously fraudulent reviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the Testimonial">
    Verify it doesn't meet publication standards.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Reject">
    Click the "Reject" button to mark it as not approved.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Status Update">
    The testimonial is marked as rejected and will not appear publicly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Both validation and rejection actions are logged with your user ID and timestamp for accountability.
</Note>

### Editing Testimonials Before Validation

If a testimonial has minor issues (typos, formatting) but is otherwise good:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Access `/testimonials/gest/edit?id={testimonial_id}`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Modify Content">
    You can edit:
    - Pseudonym (visitor's name)
    - Message text
    - Rating (1-5 stars)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Validate After Editing (Optional)">
    Check the "Validate after edit" checkbox to approve the testimonial immediately after saving your changes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit the form to update the testimonial.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use editing to fix minor typos or format issues in otherwise good testimonials. Don't make substantial changes to the visitor's message.
</Tip>

### Deleting Testimonials

To permanently remove a testimonial:

1. From the testimonial list, click "Delete"
2. Confirm the deletion
3. The testimonial is permanently removed from the database

<Warning>
  Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider rejecting instead of deleting unless the testimonial is spam or violates terms of service.
</Warning>

### Testimonial Statistics

The testimonial dashboard shows:

- **Total testimonials**: All submissions received
- **Pending**: Awaiting moderation
- **Validated**: Approved and public
- **Rejected**: Not approved
- **Average rating**: Overall visitor satisfaction score

## Updating Service Information

Depending on your permissions, you may be able to update zoo service information.

### Viewing Services

Navigate to `/cms/gest/start` to see all zoo services:

- Service name
- Description
- Type (category)
- Featured status
- Associated images

### Editing Service Details

If you have edit permissions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Service">
    Click "Edit" on the service you want to update.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Information">
    Modify:
    - Service title
    - Description
    - Link (URL for more information)
    - Type/category
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Images (Optional)">
    Upload new images if the service offering has changed:
    - Mobile image
    - Tablet image
    - Desktop image
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Submit the form. All changes are logged with your user ID.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Service updates are logged in the system. Administrators can view a complete history of changes at `/cms/gest/logs`.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Record Feeding

**Issue**: The feeding log form won't submit.

**Solutions**:
1. Verify all required fields are filled:
   - Animal selected
   - Food type chosen
   - Quantity entered (must be > 0)
   - Date provided
2. Check that you're logged in (session active)
3. Ensure you have `animal_feeding-assign` permission

### Animal Not in Dropdown

**Issue**: The animal you fed isn't in the list.

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page to reload the animal list
2. Check if the animal name is spelled differently than expected
3. Contact an administrator - the animal may not be in the system yet

### Testimonial Won't Validate

**Issue**: Click validate but the testimonial stays pending.

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page and try again
2. Check that you have Employee or Admin role
3. Verify you're logged in
4. Contact technical support if the issue persists

### Permission Denied Error

**Issue**: You get "Permission Denied" when trying to access a feature.

**Solutions**:
1. Verify your account has the Employee role
2. Contact an administrator to check your specific permissions
3. Ensure your account is active (not deactivated)

- Content is relevant to zoo experience
- Language is appropriate
- Rating matches message sentiment
- No spam or promotional content

**Reject if:**
- Inappropriate language
- Spam or advertising
- Off-topic
- Obviously fake

**Edit then approve if:**
- Minor typos or formatting issues
- Small corrections needed
- Message is otherwise appropriate
